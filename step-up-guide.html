Step Up Sign-Up Guide

Step 1

Choose the right scholarship

Most full-time private school families will start with the Private School Scholarship. Families with a qualifying diagnosis may also review Unique Abilities. PEP is generally for students who are not full-time private or public school students.

Step 2

Gather documents before applying

Have parent information, student information, proof of Florida residency, birth or age documentation, current school information, and any special eligibility documents ready before starting.

Step 3

Create or log into EMA

Go to Step Up For Students and choose Apply/Login. New families create an EMA account. Returning families should log into their existing EMA account.

Open EMA Login
Step 4

Complete and submit the application

Fill out all requested parent and student information, upload required documents, review the application carefully, and submit it before the posted deadline.

Step 5

Watch your email and EMA account

Step Up may request corrections or additional documents. Parents should check email and EMA often so the application can keep moving forward.

Step 6

Notify Lions Christian Academy after approval

Once your student is awarded, contact Lions Christian Academy so the school can help with enrollment, tuition processing, and next steps.

Email LCA
